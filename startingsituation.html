At the start of the duel you'll start with 8000 LP, or life points,and you'll draw five cards from your deck after you have decided who goes first. Your deck willbe between fourty and sixty cards, and you will have up to 15 cards in your extra deck. The player who went first will no draw for turn, and is also not allowed to enter the battle phase
